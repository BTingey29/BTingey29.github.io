Follow the Winding Road to JavaJam

We’re a little out of the way, but take a drive in the country down Copperhead Road to JavaJam today! Indulge in our locally roasted free-trade coffee and home-made pastries. You’ll feel right at home here at JavaJam!

JavaJam Coffee Bar features:

  • Easy-going live music played every night
  • Local friendly staff
  • JavaJam's very own speak-easy held three times a week
  • Delicious coffee and pastries that are made from scratch
  • Well welcomed dancing and fun
  • Open-Mic held every Friday night
